Notice on the RMUC 2025 Rules Exam

2024-11-28

Participants: All registered RMUC teams from Chinese mainland, as well as teams from Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and overseas. Tier-1 teams are not required to participate in this section and they directly hold eligibility for the regional competition of the season. But if they waive this section, they will not receive the associated product discounts. Tier-2 teams and teams from Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and overseas must participate in this section to gain submission access for the next section.

Participation: Teams participate collectively.

Exam Time: From 18:00 on December 2, 2024, to 18:00 on December 3, 2024 (Beijing Time).

Exam Link: https://djistore.wjx.cn/vm/tUwP6Dl.aspx

Exam scope:- RoboMaster 2025 University Championship Rules Manual ( https://www.robomaster.com/en-US/resource/pages/announcement/1774 ) - RoboMaster 2025 University Series Robot Building Manual ( https://www.robomaster.com/en-US/resource/pages/announcement/1774 ) - RoboMaster Battery Safety Guidelines ( https://rm-static.djicdn.com/documents/71710/764547e77e3eb1732803653018238430.pdf )

Opportunity: The exam can be attempted multiple times within the specified period.

Exam Format: 100 multiple-choice questions (randomly generated), total score of 100 points, 1 point per question.

Passing Threshold: More than 6 team members must score 96 points or higher. If you have made multiple attempts, the highest score prevails.

Benefits of passing this section:1. Receive product discounts associated with this section, as detailed in the " RoboMaster 2025 RoboMaster University Championship Participants Manual ( https://www.robomaster.com/en-US/resource/pages/announcement/1774 ) " and the " RoboMaster 2025 University Series Materials Purchase Instructions. ( https://www.robomaster.com/en-US/resource/pages/announcement/1775 ) "2. Eligibility to the season planning section.3. Sticker bonus: The top 10 teams, with any member scoring 100 points in the shortest time, will receive a set of armor module stickers.

Reminders:1. If you encounter any issues during the exam, please report them to the WeChat account (rmsaiwu). If a team is unable to complete the exam due to their own reasons, they shall take responsibility for it themselves.
