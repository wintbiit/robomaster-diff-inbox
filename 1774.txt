RMU 2025 Specifications Manuals

2024-10-21

All RoboMaster participants should abide by the rules and the relevant documents of the competition. The RoboMaster 2025 University Series (RMU 2025) includes RoboMaster University Championship (RMUC), RoboMaster University League (RMUL). The key specifications for the RMU 2024 are stated in the following documents: "Rules Manual", "Participant Manual" and "Robot Building Specifications Manual". All documents are subject to the latest version officially released by the RoboMaster Organizing Committee (hereinafter referred to as “the RMOC”).

In order to avoid ambiguities, misunderstandings or disputes over the rules, the RMOC offer a special channel for rules Q&A. If you have any questions about the event specification documents (including but not limited to the Rules Manual, Participant Manual, Robot Building Specification Manual), submit them to the RMOC and we will reply to your questions in 5 working days. Instructions for rules Q&A are as below.

1. Fill out the questionnaire and submit your questionst: https://qingflow.com/f/8n3ukbsnb001

2. The RMOC will answer the question in in 5 working days and update the the Q&A to https://qingflow.com/appView/8n3ukbsnb001/shareView/8n3ukcuvb002

To ensure the fairness and justice of the competition, the timeliness of the rule updates, and the smooth running of the competition, the RMOC will update specification documents as needed. All participants should refer to the latest version of the documents when interpreting rules. Should the Q&A contradict with the specification documents, the RMOC will mark up the contradictions and implement them in the latest specification documents. The version number of the latest document should be larger than the previous one.

For referee system-related documents, see https://www.robomaster.com/en-US/products/components/referee.

The revision log in each specification document only lists important updates. If you need to know the detailed modifications, it is recommended that participating teams use the PDF comparison tool.
