2024-10-21

1. Material Discount Eligibility

In order to support the participating teams, the RoboMaster Organizing Committee allows the participating teams that have registered for RoboMaster University Championship (RMUC), RoboMaster University League (RMUL) and passed the registration assessment to purchase materials at discounted prices. New teams that meet certain requirements can also purchase the Standard Robot Kit at affordable prices. The materials purchased at a discount can only be used as supplies for RoboMaster 2024 competitions and related events. Discount eligibility and materials purchased at discounts cannot be resold.
